this is a project in partnership with democratic systems, inc. (dsi). the goal is to create a web app prototype that can provide users with insights about their elected representatives’ legislative actions. for now, we are focusing on georgia's 117th congress and their stances regarding food security issues in the state. for my part, i was part of the backend team and was in charge of integrating the dataset we have to the frontend using an API.
