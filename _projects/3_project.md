this project is a single-player dungeon crawler game. the main objective is, as a mage, the player navigates a dungeon in total darkness. they only rely on a limited amount of light source that comes from their projectile attacks. almost forgot to mention, there are zombies in the dungeon, too. so, on top of everything else, the player needs to defeat these enemies succesfully and strategically, since they are also drawn to the light source. other core gameplay loop includes managing health, mana, and stamina bars, as well as using mage abilities like ricochet, meditation, and forcefield.
